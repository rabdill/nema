Nokomis East Mutual Aid


We're helping each other.

Nokomis East Mutual Aid is coordinating with the Nokomis East Neighborhood Association to organize neighborhood response to the ongoing civil unrest stemming from the killing of George Lloyd on May 25, 2020. There are several ways to get involved in the neighborhoods of Morris Park, Keewaydin, Minnehaha and Wenonah:

  • A Google Sheet has been started specifically for this event. It is open to the public, and people can post requests for assistance and offerse to help with the aftermath of the protests.
  • If you are uncomfortable sharing your information publicly, there are multiple private ways to contact Nokomis East Mutual Aid to request or offer assistance. See our homepage for all details. Support coordinated privately through these forms will not include participation with police.
  • The Nokomis East Mutual Aid Facebook group is open to residents and can facilitate discussions about cleanup and aid.
  • For discussions not directly related to neighborhood assistance, we ask that you please use other forums to make sure all urgent requests are visible.
