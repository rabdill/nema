Our goal is simple.

In this difficult and uncertain time, we will support our community however we can. The COVID-19 crisis has already affected all of us, and it will almost certainly get worse before it gets better.

We aren't a charity, and we certainly aren't a business—we're your neighbors, and we're here to help:

  • If it isn't safe for you to go to the grocery store, we'll go for you.
  • If you're sick and need supplies, we'll get them.
  • If you need food, we'll help you find some.
  • If you just want someone to check in every once in a while, we'd love to say hello.

There's no charge for these services, ever. Though we are in contact with local organizations, everyone at NEMA is a volunteer who lives in the neighborhood—no one knows who will need help tomorrow, so those of us who are able are helping today. If you need support and aren't sure whether NEMA can help, please get in touch!

Ask for help

There are multiple private ways to reach us. Your information will only be shared with those helping you directly.

  • Our online support form is the best way to make sure we have all the details we need to help.
  • You can email us at NokomisEastMutualAid@gmail.com.
  • You can call or text 24/7 at *tk. If there is no answer, please leave a voicemail.

Volunteer!

If you live or work in Nokomis East and want to get involved, please reach out! The online volunteer form is the quickest way to get looped in—there is no commitment involved, but filling out the form as completely as you can will connect you to all our internal communications and help us determine who can fulfill which requests. If you have questions, feel free to email us at NokomisEastMutualAid@gmail.com.
